## Purpose

DrMarchand's Laboratory is the satellite infrastructure inside Atlas.

The Laboratory exists to test, observe, document, route, and refine systems before they are promoted into the larger Design Orchard LLC ecosystem.

## Core Model

Atlas contains the Laboratory.

The Laboratory reports through Echo.

Echo routes signals toward Lionheart.

Lionheart governs execution.

Workbench displays operational state.

Atomic Notebook archives permanent evidence.

## Laboratory Roles

- Research
- Experimentation
- System testing
- API integration
- Bot testing
- Storage routing
- Evidence creation
- Architecture validation
- Pre-production staging

## Authority Rule

The Laboratory may test and propose.

The Laboratory may not authorize high-risk production actions without Lionheart approval.
