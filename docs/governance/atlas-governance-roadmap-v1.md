# Atlas Governance Roadmap v1

Date Established: 2026-06-15
Originating Author: Joseph Kyle Marchand
Business Root: Design Orchard LLC

## Mission

Create a single source of truth across clouds, repositories, business records, creative assets, and operational workflows.

The goal is governance, provenance, authority, recoverability, and long-term preservation.

## Lifecycle

Authority -> Active -> Boiler Room -> Dumpster -> Graveyard -> Ashes

## Success Condition

Atlas can answer:

1. What exists?
2. Who owns it?
3. Where is the authority?
4. Where is the backup?
5. What is its business value?
6. What is its copyright status?
7. What happens if it disappears?
