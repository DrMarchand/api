# Validation Cycle v1

Date Established: 2026-06-15
Originating Author: Joseph Kyle Marchand
Business Root: Design Orchard LLC

## Purpose

This cycle defines how Atlas separates ❌ from ✅.

The system must not treat belief, assumption, confidence, or convenience as truth.

## Cycle

👀 Curiosity  
Question, explore, inspect.

👁️‍🗨️ Unknown  
The answer is not established yet.

👁️ Clarity  
The object, claim, file, path, or system behavior is understood enough to describe.

🥽 Safety  
Risk has been reviewed and controls are active.

✅ Validated  
The claim or object passed the required check.

💯 Validated Undeniable Fact  
The claim is supported by evidence, reproducible, reviewed, and stable enough to become doctrine, policy, reference architecture, or milestone record.

## Rejection State

❌ Rejected means one or more of the following:

- unsafe
- unverified
- contradicted
- broken
- corrupted
- misleading
- not reproducible
- missing authority
- missing evidence
- premature certainty

## Big Brother Rule

🪬 Big Brother protects Atlas from arrogance.

The internal arrogance listener must never be public-facing.

Unknown is not Clarity.

Clarity is not Validation.

Validation is not Fact.

A 💯 may only be granted when evidence survives review and challenge.

## Operational Rule

No major system object may move from Unknown directly to Validated.

No policy may become official without passing through Clarity and Safety.

No milestone may be recorded as 💯 unless evidence exists in GitHub, registry, archive, or another declared authority.
