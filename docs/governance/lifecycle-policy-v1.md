# Lifecycle Policy v1

## Authority

Source of truth. May create, modify, and own records.

## Active

Current operational work. Expected to change.

## Boiler Room

Working but weird. Useful but not fully understood. Do not delete until documented.

Examples:
- bridge scripts
- migration folders
- legacy automations
- compatibility shims
- strange but functional launchers

## Dumpster

Working but disconnected or non-authoritative. May be useful temporarily.

Examples:
- duplicate exports
- old ZIPs
- migration copies
- staging folders

## Graveyard

Broken, disconnected, missing target, or dead.

Examples:
- broken aliases
- dead symlinks
- missing cloud targets
- abandoned shortcuts

## Ashes

Deleted after review, evidence, and approval.
