# Public Symbol Policy v1

Date Established: 2026-06-15
Originating Author: Joseph Kyle Marchand
Business Root: Design Orchard LLC

## Purpose

Define which governance symbols may be public and which remain internal.

## Public Symbols

👀 Curiosity
Questions, exploration, inspection.

👁️‍🗨️ Unknown
Unresolved or unmapped information.

👁️ Clarity
Information is understood enough to describe.

🥽 Safety
Risk has been reviewed and controls are active.

✅ Validated
Required checks have passed.

💯 Validated Undeniable Fact
Evidence exists, is reproducible, survives review, survives challenge, and has a declared authority.

❌ Rejected
Unsafe, unverified, contradicted, broken, corrupted, misleading, or not reproducible.

🪬 Big Brother
Anti-arrogance oversight and evidence discipline.

## Internal-Only Rule

Internal arrogance detection mechanisms are not public-facing.

Public documents may describe the function as anti-arrogance oversight, but must not expose internal watcher symbols or private listener mechanics.

## Core Rule

Unknown is not Clarity.
Clarity is not Validation.
Validation is not Fact.
