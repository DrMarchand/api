# Rhythm Agenda v1

Date Established: 2026-06-15
Originating Author: Joseph Kyle Marchand
Business Root: Design Orchard LLC

## Purpose

Rhythm Agenda is the timing, scheduling, and obligation layer for Design Orchard LLC.

It connects business rhythm to execution.

## Responsibilities

- payment due dates
- contract deadlines
- project milestones
- HubSpot deal timelines
- Asana tasks
- Google Calendar events
- Microsoft Outlook calendar events
- statement generation timing
- delivery checkpoints

## Rule

HubSpot owns business deal identity.
Asana owns task execution.
Calendars own time visibility.
Merchant systems own payment proof.
Excel owns printable payment statements.
GoDaddy stores public-safe IDs only.

Rhythm Agenda coordinates the timing between them.
