# Copyright Strategy

## Purpose

This document records the ownership and evidence strategy for original works
created under the Design Orchard LLC ecosystem by Joseph Kyle Marchand.

## Copyrightable materials

Examples:
- source code
- documentation
- diagrams
- written manifests
- original artwork
- original UI copy
- original architecture descriptions
- original training/system materials authored by Joseph Kyle Marchand

## Not copyright alone

Examples:
- names
- short phrases
- brand identifiers
- procedures
- systems
- methods
- ideas
- domain names

These may require trademark, contract, trade secret, patent, or business record
strategy instead.

## Ownership posture

Default internal posture:

- Joseph Kyle Marchand is the originating creator/operator.
- Design Orchard LLC is the business root and publication/commercial arm.
- Work produced for company operations should be documented as owned, assigned,
  or licensed to Design Orchard LLC as appropriate.

## Evidence rules

Every important work should have:

- creation date
- author/contributor
- business context
- canonical home
- Git commit or registry event
- version number where appropriate
- screenshots or exports for visual works
- no secrets in repository history

## Registration posture

High-value releases should be prepared for U.S. Copyright Office registration.
Registration is not required for copyright to exist, but registration improves
the enforcement posture and is generally required before filing a U.S. copyright
infringement lawsuit.
